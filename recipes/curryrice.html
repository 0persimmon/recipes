Curry Rice (for cuties!)

A simple cute & quick curry rice!

Description

I love quick and easy dinners and curry rice is just that! so quick! so easy! also its sooo cute! You can change the recipe easily to suit your tastes, add meat, no meat, more veggies, lots of potatoes, whatever you want!

I've listed S&B Curry roux, but you can totally substitute for another brand of your choosing. S&B also has a variety of spice levels so you can customize to your specific taste, definitely doesn't have to be spicy if you don't like spice! Goes well with pickled daikon on the side!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Cups cooked rice
  • 1 7.8oz pack of S&B Golden Curry mix (or another roux of your choosing!)
  • 1 medium onion, sliced (sweet or red is best)
  • 2 medium-sized carrots, chopped
  • 3 medium-sized gold potatoes, chopped
  • 1lb cubed chicken, pork, shrimp, etc. (optional)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil (i like avocado oil)
  • salt & pepper (to taste)
  • sliced pickled daikon (optional, topping)

Directions

  1. heat a large pot on medium heat and add cooking oil
  2. once pan is hot, add sliced onion and cook for 2 minutes or until translucent
  3. Meanwhile, cook rice according to preference (rice cooker or on the stove)
  4. add meat, if using, and cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. For shrimp, cook for just 1 minute.
  5. add carrots, potatoes, and other hard vegetables, if adding.
  6. add water, making sure it covers enitre contents. Turn heat up to high and bring water to a boil, then lower to medium and let simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes.
  7. test potatoes, making sure they are tender, then add slowly add in roux by breaking it into small chunks (about 1-2 in), stirring well after each addition. Make sure to slir thoroughly to make sure each clump of roux gets fully dissolved and mixed in!
  8. once roux is dissolved, add salt and pepper to taste and then serve over or next to rice. Enjoy!
